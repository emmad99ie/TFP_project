Metric 1 — On-Time Delivery Rate by Driver

Bars are coloured green/orange/red against a 90% target so underperformers are visible instantly. The stacked breakdown was added because early arrivals — drivers arriving before the slot opened — were a significant hidden reason the headline on-time rate was lower than expected, masking a very different problem from late arrivals.

ATA is derived as ATD − Act On Site (Est On Site used when On Site Variance = 0). Where both ATA and ATD are absent and variance = 0, ETA/ETD are used as proxies. Early = ATA < Slot From  |  On Time = ATA ≥ Slot From and ATD ≤ Slot To  |  Late = ATA > Slot From.
